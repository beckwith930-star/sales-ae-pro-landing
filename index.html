How it works

Trigger. Fan out. Draft. You send.

Every agent runs on the same loop. The shape is boring on purpose — you can read every prompt, every tool call, every artifact.

01 · Trigger

Schedule, signal, or call

Cron schedules (07:00 daily, Friday afternoon), upstream signals (a fresh earnings filing, a new job posting at a target account), or a manual run.

02 · Fan out

Across your stack

Salesforce. Gmail. Slack. LinkedIn. Web search and SEC EDGAR. Each agent uses only the tools it needs, in parallel where it can.

03 · Draft

You hit send

Every outbound artifact is a Gmail Draft, a Slack draft, or a queued LinkedIn invite. No autosent emails, no autoposted DMs. The human pulls every send trigger.

Why this works

Three rules. Held everywhere.

Drafts only

Every email, every DM, every Salesforce write goes through human approval. Nothing is autosent — by design, not by oversight.

Source-cited

Every signal cites a verifiable URL. Every recap cites a meeting. No hallucinated job postings, no invented earnings quotes.

Inspectable

Every prompt, tool call, and artifact is logged. The dashboard shows the whole run live. You can read your way through any decision.
